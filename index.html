Why should I participate in the UX training?

The three-day UX/UI training suits you if:

  • You are a product manager, business analyst, project manager, UI/UX designer, software developer, start-up founder or a business owner
  • Your team struggles with innovation, design or product management
  • You would like your team to acquire new skills in just a few days
  • You want to learn how to gain qualitative insights from your users

What is the format of our course?

Brief information about our UX training:

  • Three full days, from morning until late afternoon
  • At your location or in our Budapest headquarters
  • Modular structure – theoretical introductions followed by practice
  • Available for smaller groups (5-10 participants)
  • On-site workshops and practice

What you will learn

Collecting user insights

We will teach you how to systematically learn about users and how to apply collected data in a design process.

Lean UX process planning

Get to know how users navigate through your website and build a powerful journey to improve online customer experience.

User testing and prototyping

Learn prototyping and testing to discover the best design layout that would be clear for your users.

Screen design

Paper-based, low/high fidelity wireframes, visual hierarchy, intro to Axure and much more.

UX research methods

Get to know your users better through fieldwork, A/B tests, interviews, five-second test and more.

UX writing and microcopy

Create a powerful message with the right words to make your users understad your business and value proposition.
